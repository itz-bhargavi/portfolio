Here your Journey Begins, What you want to become in your Life

After Class 10, students in many education systems have to choose a stream based on their interests and career goals.
Here's an overview of the common streams and the options available after Class 10 in India:

Science Stream Students

The Science stream is one of the most popular choices for students who are interested in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. It opens doors to a wide range of career options, especially in fields related to technology, medicine, engineering, research, and more.

This stream is for students interested in fields related to engineering, technology, and physical sciences. The focus here is on Mathematics and physical sciences (Physics and Chemistry), which are essential for various technical and engineering careers.

Subjects Offered:

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Physical Education
  • Computer Science

Popular Career Options for Non-Medical Science Stream:

  • Engineering (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Computer Science, etc.)
  • Architecture
  • Data Science/AI
  • Software Development/Programming
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Cybersecurity
  • Environmental Science
  • Space Science & Research
  • Defense (Technical Branch, DRDO, ISRO)

Subjects Offered:

  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Physical Education

Popular Career Options for Medical Science Stream:

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)
  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Nursing
  • Physiotherapy
  • Biomedical Science
  • Biotechnology
  • Genetics
  • Medical Research
  • Veterinary Science
  • Public Health (MPH)

This stream is for students who are interested in pursuing careers in the medical and healthcare fields. The core subjects in this stream focus on Biology and related sciences.

Commerce Stream Students

The Commerce stream is suitable for students interested in business, finance, economics, and accounts. It’s a versatile field that opens up numerous career paths in finance, business management, economics, and entrepreneurship.

Subjects Offered:

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Mathematic
  • Entrepreneurship

Popular Career Options for Commerce Stream:

  • Chartered Accountant (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Financial Analyst
  • Business Management
  • Banking
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Economist
  • Hotel Management
  • Event Management
  • Fashion Management

Humanities (Arts) Stream

Subjects Offered:

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Geography
  • Economics
  • Fine Arts

Popular Career Options for Humanities (Arts) Stream:

  • Law (LLB)
  • Journalism
  • Psychology
  • Sociology
  • Human Rights
  • Public Administration
  • Teaching
  • Design (Fashion Design, Graphic Design, etc.)
  • Event Management
  • Social Work
  • Content Writing

The Humanities or Arts stream is often chosen by students with interests in subjects like History, Political Science, Literature, Sociology, Psychology, and more. It offers a broad spectrum of career opportunities, especially in fields like law, design, journalism, and social work.
